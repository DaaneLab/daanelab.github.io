Future of genomic epidemiology

The COVID-19 pandemic has pushed the field perhaps ~5 years into the future

  • Ebola in West Africa: 28,646 confirmed cases and 1610 genomes
  • Zika in the Americas: 223,477 confirmed cases and 942 genomes
  • Ebola in DRC: 3300 confirmed cases and 792 genomes
  • 2018/2019 seasonal flu in US: 290k confirmed cases and 8864 genomes
  • Global SARS-CoV-2 pandemic: 162M confirmed cases and 1.6M genomes
